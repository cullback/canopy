Strategy for catan.

- what resource(s) are scarce
- what resource(s) are bountiful
- wheat, sheep
- are any strong resources near a compatible port?
- what are the top 4-5 spots
- given my first placement, what strategy should i play?
- is the layout of the board shaped in such a way that certain resources can be easily monopolized (or at the very least, i can get cut off from)?
- will i need a port given scarcity of some resources?
- which resource(s) could i get fairly easily through a trade?

## Placements

- Ore Wins
- Production, Production, Production
- No Wheat = Defeat
- Get All Five Resources
- Ore/Wheat/Sheep is Still Very Strong
- Good Ratio of Wood:Brick is 1:1
- Good Ratio of Ore:Wheat:Sheep is 5:3:2
- Think About Ports / Third Settlement
- Point Roads to Coasts or Desert
- Don't Be Greedy (7-8pt Spots Will Be Contested)
- Free Road? Yes, Please
- Same Numbers Mean Feast or Famine
- When First, Check Edges
- When Third, Take Rare Resources
- When Third, Predict Fourth
- When Second, Predict Last

## Gameplay

- Know Thyself: Army or Road?
- Know Others: Army or Road?
- Track Army & Road Progress
- Largest Army Better Than Longest Road
- Build Sooner / Take Bird in the Hand
- Spread Out Production
- Avoid Confrontations
- Get to a Port
- Don't Make Yourself a Target
- When Ahead, Buy Dev. Cards
- When Behind, Build Cities
- Avoid Taking Army Too Early
- But Never Take Army Too Late
- Take Longest Road As Late as Possible
- Know Your Win Condition(s)

## Development Cards

- Use Knights Before Rolling (Except When Holding 7 Cards)
- Save Monopoly for the End
- Beware of Monopoly
- Road Building? Plan Ahead
- Use Invention for Rare Resources
- Track Dev. Cards Remaining

## Trading

- Trade Before Building
- Trade Freely on Your Turn
- Trade Freely for Rare Resources
- Trade Cautiously Otherwise
- Don't Trade if it Takes You Over 7 Cards
- Demand 2:1 if You Have Over 7 Cards
- 48% Chance 7 Doesn't Roll

## Robber

- Block/Rob the Leader\* \*Discount Longest Road
- Block Resources You Produce
- Unfair Robber? Play Tit for Tat
- Racing? Rob & Block What He Needs to Win Race

## Table Management

- Table Talk Works
- Don't Be a Jerk
- Don't Show-Off
